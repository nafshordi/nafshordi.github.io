Mann Fest: Happy Birthday Robb Mann!!

2015-12-07

This weekend at Mann Fest, we celebrated the contributions of our colleague Robb Mann, on the occasion of his 60th birthday. Robb is very prolific, both in producing innovative scientific research, but also in training excellent undergraduate and graduate students. I met several of them during Mann Fest, who have now scattered across the globe, many as successful professors, and are engaged in diverse and exciting research. Not surprisingly, Robb is also an extraordinary lecturer. You can find my talk at Mann Fest here.
