\cvsection{Profile}
Niayesh Afshordi is an astrophysicist working at the interface of cosmology,
gravitation, quantum theory, and observation. His research connects fundamental
questions to gravitational-wave data, black holes, the cosmic microwave
background, large-scale structure, compact objects, and other astrophysical
probes. Current themes include quantum black holes and quadratic gravity,
strong-gravity tests, early-universe cosmology, dark matter and dark energy,
missing baryons, and modified gravity.
His interdisciplinary work also includes medical-physics applications,
radiotherapy, and epidemiological and public-health modelling.
