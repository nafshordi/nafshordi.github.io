\cvsection{Profile}
Niayesh Afshordi is an astrophysicist whose research connects fundamental questions in gravity and quantum physics to observations of the universe. His work spans black holes, gravitational waves, the early universe, the cosmic microwave background, dark matter and dark energy, cosmic structure, and observational tests of gravity.
