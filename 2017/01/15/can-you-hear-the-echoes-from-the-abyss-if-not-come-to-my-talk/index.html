Can you hear the echoes from the abyss? If not, come to my talk!

2017-01-15

Image Credit: NASA/Ames Research Center/C. Henze Recently, with Jahed Abedi and Hannah Dykaar, we found gravitational wave signatures for quantum gravity effects near black hole horizons. If you'd like to hear more about this, and you're in Waterloo, come to my seminar tomorrow:
  • Jan. 16 (Mon), 10am, in MC6460 (Univ. of Waterloo), Prof. Niayesh Afshordi (UW, PI)

In a second paper, which just appeared on arXiv, we clarify some of the misunderstandings about our finding. Delayed echoes from Planck-scale structure near black hole horizons predict a peak at x=1 in this plot. As can be seen, analysis of LIGO gravitational wave data indeed shows such a peak with >99% confidence level (from arXiv:1701.03485)
